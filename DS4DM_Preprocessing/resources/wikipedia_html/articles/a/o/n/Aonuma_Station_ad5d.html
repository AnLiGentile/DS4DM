Aonuma Station

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Aonuma Station (青沼駅, Aonuma-eki?) is a railway station on the Koumi Line, East Japan Railway Company (JR East), in Irisawa in the city of Saku, Nagano Prefecture, Japan.
